Margarita

Needs no introduction.

Ingredients

  • 2oz tequilla
  • 1oz lime juice
  • 1oz Cointreau
  • .25oz simple syrup

Recipe

  1. Shake tequila, lime juice, simple syrup, and Cointreau in cocktail shaker with ice until well chilled.
  2. Rim a coupe glass with salt.
  3. Strain into glass.
