Manhattan

For when you want to feel like you're taking in a drink in the Big Apple.

Ingredients

  • 2oz bourbon or rye
  • 1oz sweet vermouth
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters

Recipe

  1. Add ingredients to a mixing glass with ice and stir until well chilled.
  2. Pour into a chilled coupe glass.
Top with a Maraschino cherry.
