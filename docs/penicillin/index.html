Penicillin

The perfect hangover cure.

Ingredients

  • 2oz blended scotch
  • .75oz lemon juice
  • .5oz honey/ginger syrup
  • .25oz Islay Single Malt scotch

Recipe

  1. Shake blended scotch, lemon juice, and syrup in a cocktail shaker with ice until well chilled.
  2. Strain drink into rocks glass over ice.
  3. Top with Islay scotch (pour over the back of a spoon).
