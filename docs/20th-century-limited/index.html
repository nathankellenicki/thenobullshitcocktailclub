Originally served on the train that ran between NYC and Chicago in the first half of the 20th century, incidentally named the 20th Century Limited.

Ingredients

  • 1.5oz gin
  • .75oz Lillet Blanc
  • .5oz creme de cacao
  • .75oz lemon juice

Recipe

  1. Shake Lillet Blanc, creme de cacao, lemon juice, and gin in cocktail shaker with ice until well chilled.
  2. Strain into a chilled coupe glass.
As a bonus, garnish with a lemon twist.
