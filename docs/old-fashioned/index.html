Old Fashioned

The Old Fashioned. The quintessential Bourbon drink and one of the oldest cocktails in the world.

Ingredients

  • 2oz bourbon or rye
  • 1tsp demerara syrup
  • 2 dashes Angustura bitters

Recipe

  1. Add demerara syrup, Angostura bitters, and bourbon to a rocks glass.
  2. Add ice. A large rock works best. Stir until well chilled.
  3. Spritz an orange peel into the glass and drop the peel in.
As a bonus, drop in a Maraschino cherry on a cocktail stick.
