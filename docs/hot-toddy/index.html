Hot Toddy

Depending on whom you ask, it's either a warming winter drink, or the perfect cure for the common cold.

Ingredients

  • 2oz Scotch whisky
  • .5oz lemon juice
  • .5oz maple syrup
  • 4oz boiling hot water
  • 1 English tea bag
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 4 cloves

Recipe

  1. Place the tea bag and a cinnamon stick in the bottom of a rocks glass.
  2. Add lemon juice, maple syrup, whisky, and hot water, in that order.
  3. Add cloves. Remove after a 2 minutes. (Alternatively, place cloves into a lemon wheel in the glass.)
If you don't have maple syrup, honey will do fine.
