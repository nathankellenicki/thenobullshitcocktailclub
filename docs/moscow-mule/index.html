Moscow Mule

Not actually invented in Moscow. Or anywhere else in Russia.

Ingredients

  • 2oz vodka
  • .75oz lime juice
  • 5oz ginger beer

Recipe

  1. Fill a mule cup with ice.
  2. Add vodka, lime juice, and ginger beer.
  3. Stir thoroughly until mixed.
Ginger beer is important. Don't use ginger ale.
