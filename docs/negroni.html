Composed of gin, sweet vermouth, and Campari, the Negroni is the classic three-ingredient cocktail.

Ingredients

  • 2oz gin
  • 1oz sweet vermouth
  • 0.75oz Campari

Recipe

  1. Shake gin, sweet vermouth, and Campari in cocktail shaker with ice until well chilled.
  2. Strain into a rocks glass over a large rock.
As a bonus, drop in a Maraschino cherry on a cocktail stick.
