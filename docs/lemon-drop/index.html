Created in San Francisco in the '70s, the Lemon Drop is a deliciously boozy yet tart concoction which is easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 2oz vodka
  • .5oz triple sec
  • 1oz lemon juice
  • 1oz simple syrup

Recipe

  1. Coat the rim of a martini glass with lemon juice, dip in sugar, and let dry.
  2. Shake vodka, triple sec, lemon juice, and simple syrup in cocktail shaker with ice until well chilled.
  3. Strain into the martini glass.
As a bonus, garnish with a slice of lemon on the rim.
