Spicy Tequila Sour

A sour and spicy take on the Margarita.

Ingredients

  • 1 fresh jalape√±o
  • .5oz simple syrup
  • 1oz lime juice
  • 1oz pineapple juice
  • 2oz tequila

Recipe

  1. Cut three small slices of jalapeno and muddle in the bottom of a cocktail shaker with simple syrup.
  2. Add lime juice, pineapple juice, tequila, and egg white.
  3. Dry shake for 20 seconds.
  4. Add ice and shake for a further 20 seconds.
  5. Serve in a coupe glass.
Garnish with a slice of jalapeno.
