Daquiri

The traditional three-ingredient Cuban drink.

Ingredients

  • 2oz light rum
  • 1oz lime juice
  • .75oz demerara syrup

Recipe

  1. Shake rum, lime juice, and demerara syrup in cocktail shaker with ice until well chilled.
  2. Strain into a chilled coupe glass.
As a bonus, garnish with a lime twist.
