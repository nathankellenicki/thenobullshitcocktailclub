Cucumber Martini

A fresh, summery twist on a martini.

Ingredients

  • 1 cucumber
  • .5oz simple syrup
  • Pinch of salt
  • .75oz lemon juice
  • 3oz gin

Recipe

  1. Cut a handful of cucumber cubes and muddle in a cocktail shaker with simple syrup and a pinch of salt.
  2. Add lemon juice and gin, shake in a cocktail shaker with ice.
  3. Serve in a coupe glass.
Garnish with a slice of cucumber o the rim.
