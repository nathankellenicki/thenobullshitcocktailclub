Vesper Martini

Despite what 007 says, under no circumstances should this be shaken and not stirred.

Ingredients

  • 3oz gin
  • 1oz vodka
  • .5oz Lillet Blanc

Recipe

  1. Add ingredients to a mixing glass with ice and stir until well chilled.
  2. Strain into a coupe or martini glass.
  3. Spritz lemon twist into glass, rub along rim, and drop into cocktail.
